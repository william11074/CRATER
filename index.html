03

Pothole Repair

Cheaply, quickly, and efficiently patch potholes with our automatic repair system. Reduces backlogging of potholes and allows for a more efficient repair system. With our system's help, cities can shift their attention to other construction projects.

There is currently a backlog of roadway repair work in the United States, making automomous solutions nessecary.

04

From Design To Real Life

We have designed a simple and inexpensive system which can be mounted on self-driving trucks in order to repair these potholes. It consists of a container to hold cold-mix asphalt, a dispensing system, and a roller which smooths over the patched pothole.
